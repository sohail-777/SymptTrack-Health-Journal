About SymptTrack

SymptTrack is an interactive health journal that allows you to record daily symptoms and receive a suggested diagnosis generated by a clinical diagnosis model (CORe). Entries are stored locally in your browser's localStorage.

How to use

  1. Login or continue as guest.
  2. Open Symptoms — select a month and enter your symptoms for a day into a box.
  3. Click Submit to send the symptoms to the backend. The backend runs the CORe model to produce possible ICD9 labels.
  4. The diagnosis will appear below the submit button and the entry will be saved locally. Use Save (local) to save without calling the backend.
  5. Open Records to download a PDF of the month or browse previous entries.

Privacy note

All symptom entries are stored in your browser only (localStorage). The CORe model inference is performed on the server backend.
